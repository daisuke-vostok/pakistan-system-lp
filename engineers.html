FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Do I need to speak Japanese?

No. All client communication is handled by Japan System's bilingual hub. You work in English.

Can I keep my other clients / Upwork profile?

Yes. Standard engagements are non-exclusive. Dedicated (exclusive) lab roles are agreed separately.

Am I employed by Japan System or the Japanese company?

Neither. You're an independent contractor working with Japan System, responsible for your own taxes in Pakistan. The Japanese client is Japan System's customer — not your employer.

What if the client messages me directly?

Politely redirect them to the Japan System hub. All instructions flow through us — this protects you and the project.

How and when am I paid?

Monthly in USD via international transfer (e.g., Wise / Payoneer), per the rate in your offer. The trial is paid too.

What happens between projects?

You stay in our talent pool — no unpaid obligation. Take other work freely; we reach out when a matching project opens.

A note on recruitment fraud

Japan System will never ask you for money, fees, or financial details (card numbers, account passwords) during application and interviews. Our trial tasks are paid — never the other way around. Payout details are collected only after you sign your contract. If anyone claiming to represent Japan System asks you for payment, it's a scam — report it to us.
