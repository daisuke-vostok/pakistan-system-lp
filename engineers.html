FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Do I need to speak Japanese?

No. All client communication is handled by Vostok's bilingual hub. You work in English.

Can I keep my other clients / Upwork profile?

Yes. Standard engagements are non-exclusive. Dedicated (exclusive) lab roles are agreed separately.

Am I employed by Vostok or the Japanese company?

Neither. You're an independent contractor working with Vostok, responsible for your own taxes in Pakistan. The Japanese client is Vostok's customer — not your employer.

What if the client messages me directly?

Politely redirect them to the Vostok hub. All instructions flow through us — this protects you and the project.

How and when am I paid?

Monthly in USD via international transfer (e.g., Wise / Payoneer), per the rate in your offer. The trial is paid too.

What happens between projects?

You stay in our talent pool — no unpaid obligation. Take other work freely; we reach out when a matching project opens.

A note on recruitment fraud

Vostok will never ask you for money, fees, or financial details (card numbers, account passwords) during application and interviews. Our trial tasks are paid — never the other way around. Payout details are collected only after you sign your contract. If anyone claiming to represent Vostok asks you for payment, it's a scam — report it to us.
